Olin Electric Motorsports 2018-2019

Collaborating to Build an Electric Vehicle

My third year on the team, another student and I changed what cells we used for our battery pack as well as the battery pack enclosure material. We redesigned the segment, using two polycarbonate sheets and a structural PCB that measures the temperature and voltage of the cells to retain the cels. I also designed the lid of the accumulator, which disconnected all the segments from each other to ensure that whenever the lid was off, the pack was never connected and making the pack more safe for serviceability. At Formula North, a competion run by FSAE in Ontario, we passed accumulator technical inspection!
